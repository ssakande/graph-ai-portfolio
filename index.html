About Me

           
               
                                   
               
                   

                        I am a results-driven professional with over 10 years of experience in information management and monitoring & evaluation within the health and education sectors, particularly in humanitarian settings. My academic background includes a Master of Public Health in Quantitative Methods (Biostatistics), a Master's degree in Statistics, and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics-Physics.

My expertise spans data consolidation, quality improvement, statistical analysis, and data visualization to inform strategic decisions. I am committed to capacity building, knowledge sharing, and fostering an inclusive environment.

About the Course: Generative AI for Work & Research

           
               

                    This "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity" course, offered by The GRAPH Courses, is an 8-week live cohort program focused on "No-Code AI Tools for Task Automation". It's designed to equip professionals and researchers with practical skills to leverage AI for enhanced productivity across various domains.                

               

Course Features:

               
                       
  • Weekly live workshops and help sessions
    •                    
  • Private community forums for collaboration
    •                    
  • Personalized feedback on assessments
    •                    
  • Capstone project coaching
    •                    
  • Shareable certificate upon completion
    •                    
  • Lifetime access to a tutorial library
    •                
               

Course Modules:

               
                   
                       

Module 1: Websites, Documents & Presentations

                       

Learn about LLMs, generating Markdown, Latex, and HTML, building simple research websites, and using AI presentation tools.

                   
                   
                       

Module 2: AI-Automated Data Analysis

                       

Master AI tools for data analysis, file manipulation, and automated report creation (e.g., ChatGPT Code Interpreter, TheBricks).

                   
                   
                       

Module 3: GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

                       

Explore AI techniques for literature search, text analysis, knowledge synthesis, and extracting insights from large text collections.

                   
                   
                       

Module 4: Custom GPTs and Chatbots

                       

Dive into building research assistants, understanding LLM architecture, context windows, RAG systems, and integrating AI assistants.

                   
                   
                       

Module 5: AI for Task Automation

                       

Discover strategies for automating repetitive research tasks, setting up email response automations, and designing AI agents.

                   
                   
                       

Module 6: GenAI for Communication and Outreach

                       

Explore AI-driven approaches for research dissemination, creating summaries, scientific figures, video abstracts, and scheduling content.

                   
                   
                       

Module 7: Capstone Project & Showcase

                       

Collaborate to create a final AI solution, tool, or presentation, receive feedback, and present projects to peers and guests.
